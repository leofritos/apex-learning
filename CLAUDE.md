# Apex Learning Project

## Who I Am
Salesforce Administrator at DealerTire with hands-on org experience.
No formal development background, but comfortable with config, flows, and validation rules.

## Goal
Pass the Salesforce Platform Developer I (PD1) exam.

## Learning Sequence
1. Variables & data types
2. Collections (List, Set, Map)
3. SOQL & SOSL
4. Classes & methods
5. DML operations & bulkification
6. Triggers
7. Test classes & code coverage

## Learning Approach
- Ask me questions before explaining — don't give me the answer first
- Help me understand *why*, not just *what*
- When I write code, point out what I got right before correcting mistakes
- Connect new concepts back to Salesforce config I already know (e.g. "this is like a formula field, but...")
- Keep examples relevant to objects I'd know: Accounts, Opportunities, Cases

## Current Focus
Variables & data types
